MSME Gateway
One-Stop Portal for Business
The Digital Gateway is a centralised information platform for Micro, Small, Medium, Enterprises (MSME) in Ghana. It serves as a one-stop business advisory platform, to assist MSMEs find timely, relevant, and credible information to manage challenges related to financial, market, operations, and external factors. Businesses will discover reliable business-related information to deal with common problems and issues through the provision of business advisory and information, digital marketing
The following information is available on the platform.
  • Government Policy on MSME and information on Regulations/Standards
  • Financing and Financial Information
  • Taxation Incentives, Subsidies
  • Advertising Information
  • Technical Support, Training and Capacity Building
  • Information on inputs including Raw Materials and Machinery
  • Business Development, Advisory and Investment Opportunities
  • Product and Market Information
  • Export and Import Trade-related including AfCFTA
  • Security and safety
  • The digital gateway has been designed to enable access and sharing of useful information for business owners as well as or policy/decision makers and researchers. Business can share experiences in trading, operating their businesses or challenges faced at the multiple land crossing border points, airports and ports.
